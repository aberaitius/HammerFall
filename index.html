Explanation of Terms

  • Total Expense: The total amount you are willing to spend.
  • Buyer's Premium: A percentage fee paid by the buyer to the auction house, calculated on the hammer price.
  • Tax Rate on Hammer Price: The percentage of tax applied to the hammer price.
  • Tax Rate on Buyer's Premium: The percentage of tax applied to the buyer's premium.
  • Tax Rate on Charges: The percentage of tax applied to additional charges.
